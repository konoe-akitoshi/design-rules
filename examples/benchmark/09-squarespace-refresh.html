Refresh is the season Squarespace ships everything new at once — and this year it lands in two parts. First, the way a site is designed: Blueprint AI to start one from a conversation, and a new Finish Layer of pro-grade controls to make it move.

Chapter I · Plate 01

Design faster with Blueprint AI

New ways to start a website, with content curated just for you — build via chat, or generate a set of adaptive templates.

  • —Blueprint templates

    Choose from designer-made templates pre-filled with curated AI content and styling aligned with your brand.

  • —Chat within Blueprint AI

    Build through a simple, conversational flow that generates a personalized design with brand-specific content and layouts.

  • —Squarespace GPT

    Build a site directly in ChatGPT through a guided flow that adapts content to your brand.

  • —AI image backgrounds

    AI-generated image backgrounds made for text overlay, curated for a cohesive, professional look.

  • —AI video backgrounds

    Add AI-generated video backgrounds to keep visitors engaged and add variety to your page design.

Chapter I · Plate 02

Finish Layer

Transform your website design into an immersive experience with professional-grade customization and editing controls.

  • —Block animations

    Drive stronger engagement with interactive, highly customizable animation treatments.

  • —Block transforms

    Add depth and personality with next-generation display and placement flexibility.

  • —Responsive editing

    Flexible new editing logic that further optimizes your design for mobile breakpoints.

  • —Font uploads

    Import external font files to fine-tune styling and maintain brand consistency.

  • —Block hiding

    Show or hide individual blocks per breakpoint for cleaner responsive layouts.

  • —Stacks

    Group and arrange blocks together so complex sections stay easy to manage.

  • —Mobile overrides

    Adjust spacing and layout on mobile without touching the desktop view.

  • —Account-wide saved sections

    Save and reuse sections across every site in your account.
