006FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can I request changes during the process?

Yes — you're free to ask for anything. But the sprint stays capped at 14 days. Any revisions or direction changes may impact what can be delivered within that timeframe.

What if I need something beyond the core identity?

This offer includes a complete brand system, but if you need more — like motion, a website, or packaging — we're open to extending the work after delivery. This is where every brand starts.

Do I own everything that's created?

Absolutely. You get a complete, organized brand package: logo files (primary + alternates), typography pairings, color palettes, social templates, brand guidelines, and more — all export-ready and yours.

Is this right for early-stage startups?

Absolutely. This offer was made for founders who move fast and need their brand to do the same — no delays, no meetings that could've been emails.

How do we get started?

You can either book the sprint directly or schedule a call first. Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive a short form to clarify your brand's direction and values — then we begin.
