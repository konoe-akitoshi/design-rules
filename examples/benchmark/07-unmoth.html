FAQ 006

Can I request changes during the process?

Yes — you're free to ask for anything. But the sprint remains capped at 14 days. Any revisions or direction changes may impact what can be delivered within that time. We recommend making clear decisions early to get the most out of the process.

What if I need something beyond the core identity?

This offer includes a complete brand system but if you need more — like motion, website, or packaging — we can discuss that after delivery.

Do I own everything that's created?

Yes. You receive full rights to every asset we deliver. No licenses, no limitations.

What's included in the delivery?

A complete, organized brand package. That means logo files (primary + alternates), typography pairings, color palettes, social templates, brand guidelines, and more — all export-ready.

How do we get started?

You can either book the sprint directly or schedule a call first. Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive a short form to clarify your brand's direction and values — then we begin.

Is this right for early-stage startups?

Absolutely. This offer was made for founders who move fast and need their brand to do the same — no delays, no meetings that could've been emails.
