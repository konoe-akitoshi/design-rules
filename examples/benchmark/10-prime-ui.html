// faq

Your questions, answered.

01What kind of sites can I build with Prime UI?

Marketing landing pages, SaaS apps, documentation, portfolios and blogs. Anything you'd build with Next.js and Tailwind — Prime UI just gives you a production-grade head start and keeps the code consistent as it grows.

02Does Prime UI generate the full design for my site?

It assembles real layouts from a designed component system, so the output is coherent rather than random. You start from a sitemap or wireframe, tune it with direct controls, then export — no prompt-engineering and no mess to clean up.

03How does licensing work?

It's a one-time fee, not a subscription. Personal covers one project; Business covers unlimited client projects and a team of up to ten. Once you've exported, the code is yours to own, modify and ship.

04Do you work with agencies?

Yes. The Business license is built for agencies and studios shipping multiple client sites — unlimited projects, team seats and priority support. Get in touch if you need a larger seat count.

05What support do you offer?

Documentation and a CLI reference cover the common path. Personal includes community support; Business adds priority email support with a one-business-day response target.
