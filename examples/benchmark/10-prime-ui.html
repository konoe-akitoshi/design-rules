FAQ

Your questions, answered.

Who is Prime UI for?

Founders, indie hackers, designers, and agencies who want a production-quality marketing site without wiring up components from scratch. If you can describe the site, Prime UI builds the foundation.

What kind of sites can I build with Prime UI?

Marketing and content sites: landing pages, blogs, documentation, pricing, and changelogs — anything that ships as static or server-rendered Next.js with Tailwind CSS.

Who built Prime UI?

A small team of engineers and designers who spent 10+ years building marketing sites. Prime UI is that experience, encoded into an intelligent system.

Does Prime UI generate the full design for my site?

It generates a complete, balanced starting point — structure, sections, and styling. You stay in control and refine from a first draft that already looks good.

Which plan is best for me?

Solo builders take Personal. Teams shipping multiple sites take Business for unlimited seats and a private Slack channel. Both include unlimited projects.

How does licensing work?

A one-time fee, no subscription. Personal covers a single user; Business covers your whole team. Build unlimited projects on either license.

Do you work with agencies?

Yes — Business is built for agencies running many client projects. Need a custom setup or volume terms? Contact us and we'll tailor it.

What support do you offer?

Personal includes standard email support. Business adds a private Slack channel with direct access to the team.
