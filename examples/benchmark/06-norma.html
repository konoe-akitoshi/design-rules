Questions, answered.

What does Norma actually do? +
Norma blocks the apps you select and their notifications. When you scan the Unit Disc with your iPhone, the apps you chose become inaccessible and their notifications stop — scan again to restore access.
Does it work on Android? +
No. Norma currently works with iPhone only.
Does it require a subscription or battery? +
No — no subscription and no battery. Nothing to charge, no recurring fee, and no hidden cost.
How does it connect to my phone? +
Norma uses NFC. To activate it, place the top of your iPhone — near the camera area — against the disc. Heavy or thick cases can sometimes interfere with the signal and make the connection a little weaker; that’s normal.
Will it slow down my phone or drain the battery? +
No. Norma works through your iPhone’s built-in Screen Time, and nothing runs in the background — so there’s no performance hit and no extra battery drain.
Can I customize which apps are blocked? +
During setup, you choose which apps Norma will control. You can change it at any time by switching between different Focus modes directly from the app’s home screen.
Can I block websites, not just apps? +
Yes — you can block distracting websites alongside apps, all grouped into your presets.
What happens if I lose the disc? +
Norma includes a limited number of emergency unblocks you can use if you don’t have your disc on hand.
