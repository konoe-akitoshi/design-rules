FAQ

Answers to
your questions

Everything else you might be wondering before you open your first pool.

Does Pool charge fees?

No. Pool does not charge monthly fees and never takes a percentage of the money you collect. You keep every cent that goes into your pool.

How does Pool make money?

Pool earns a share of the interchange paid by merchants when you spend with the Pool card, plus interest on funds held at our partner bank — not from your contributions.

Is Pool a bank? Are my funds FDIC-insured?

Pool is a financial technology company, not a bank. Banking services are provided by our partner bank, Member FDIC, where eligible deposits are insured up to $250,000 per depositor.

How is Pool different from Venmo, Cash App or Splitwise?

Splitting apps track who owes whom across personal balances. Pool is a real shared account: the money actually lives together, everyone can see it, and you spend directly from it with a card — no IOUs and no reimbursements.

How can I get in touch?

Email our team at hello@poolmoney.com. We'd love to hear from you.
