● FAQs

Not AI-gen answers.
Real ones here.

What can AI FOURMULA create for my brand?

Product images, fashion shots, lifestyle scenes and short videos for PDPs, social, ads and email.

What do I need to start?

Just a store link or a product photo. We use it to generate on-brand visuals automatically.

Will the visuals match our brand look?

Yes. We learn from your existing images and keep colours, lighting and style consistent.

Do I own the assets you generate?

Yes. All approved outputs are yours to use across any channel.

How fast do I get results?

Usually in seconds or a few minutes, depending on how many assets you request.

Does this replace my creative team?

No. It speeds up production so your team can focus on ideas, not manual reshoots and cropping.
